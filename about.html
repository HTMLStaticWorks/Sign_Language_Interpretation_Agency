About ClearHands

Breaking Barriers, Building Connections

We believe communication is a fundamental human right. ClearHands delivers premium, fully compliant sign language interpretation to empower individuals and organizations alike.

Our Purpose

Empowering the Deaf Community

ClearHands provides RID-certified, culturally competent interpreters for healthcare, legal proceedings, educational platforms, and corporate environments. We combine professional expertise with advanced scheduling technology to make language access frictionless.

Our Workflow

How We Deliver Access

1. Request

Schedule an interpreter via our online portal or connect instantly for VRI.

2. Match

Our coordinators match your assignment with a certified specialist in your industry.

3. Interpret

The session is completed on-site or via secure high-definition VRI streaming.

4. Verify

Review logs, confirm compliance reports, and retrieve billing details.
