Proactive Accessibility

ADA & Section 508 Compliance

Federal and state regulations require organizations, clinics, and government bodies to provide communication access for Deaf and hard-of-hearing individuals. We help you meet these requirements seamlessly.

Americans with Disabilities Act (ADA)

Compliance with Titles II and III, ensuring standard auxiliary communication aids are active in public and commercial facilities.

Ensuring federal agencies and institutions with federal funding provide accessible digital portals and public streams.
